Message from the Head of School

A very warm welcome to Sreenidhi International School! The school is unique in Hyderabad and India. The quality of education provided is quality assured by CIS and NEASC which gives peace of mind to parents that children are safe, well-educated and in a stable and high-quality environment. In addition, the school proudly offers a continuum of IB (International Baccalaureate) education with its PYP (Primary Years Programme), MYP (Middle Years Programme) and DP (Diploma Programme).

Sreenidhi is one of those rare schools you find in the international school environment, where it really walks the walk! There is much talk around the world about the mission, vision, global citizenship and student empowerment, but at Sreenidhi you can really see it, feel it and enjoy it. I have never been in a school that feels so warm and welcoming and that actually provides students with the opportunities and learning as outlined in the school mission. Please visit us on our gorgeous campus with empathetic, resilient and empowered young people.

'Malcolm Nicolson'
