Welcome to Sreenidhi International School!

Our campus spans 60 acres with outstanding architecture in Aziz Nagar. We offer day boarding, weekly boarding, and full boarding options. Our Enrolments Team is experienced and here to guide you through the admissions process.

We welcome around 200+ students each year, creating a diverse community of approximately 700 students and dedicated staff. Our IB examination results are excellent, leading to scholarships and a world-class education at Sreenidhi International School.

We value potential and a positive attitude in our students, regardless of their educational background. Entrance exams are required for MYP & DP levels.

Boarding at Sreenidhi

Boarding at Sreenidhi is where your child will become a part of the 24 hours, 7 days a week safe and life-long learning community where they grow up to be global citizens. They will be well taken care of by our experienced dorm parents and teachers and surrounded by their friends, peers and staff.

🏠

Dorm Facility

Every student is given a well-ventilated cubicle space with inbuilt desk, bed, cupboard and storage. The entire building is centrally air conditioned. Capacity for 72 boarders across four wings.

🎯

Extra-Curricular

Dance, Drama, Music, Art, Football, Basketball, Tennis, Squash, Cricket, Table Tennis, Badminton, Carrom and Chess. Yoga teachers help students maintain physical and mental well-being.

📅

5-Day & 7-Day Boarding

Five-day boarders leave on Friday evening and return Monday. Seven-day boarders schedule extra-curriculars and academic support with access to our huge library and high-speed WiFi.

Alumni

The Sreenidhi International School Alumni Association (SISAA) helps graduated students stay connected with their alma mater and network with their fellow members, staff and management. The alumni actively take part in all the alumni events and reunions, creating a special bond with the school community.

Whether it is 10 days or 10 years ago that a Sreenidhian graduated, SISAA provides a plethora of benefits that pay dividends on their degree for life!

Work at Sreenidhi

Why Work for Us?

Sreenidhi International School aims to attract the best teachers and administrative staff to provide the community with the best centre for excellent learning and teaching. SIS faculty is well qualified, trained and has a wide range of experience, some with significant IB school experience around the world. A couple of our faculty are IB workshop leaders, trainers or IB examiners.

We are committed to attracting, developing and retaining the best suitable faculty from around the world to ensure the very best in the delivery of excellent learning and teaching to our students.
