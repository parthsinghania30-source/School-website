Primary Years Programme (PYP)
Ages 3–11

About the Programme

The Primary Years Programme of SIS fosters the social and emotional growth of children in an environment that intertwines academics and enrichment activities. Our approach helps students understand relationships between disciplines and apply what they have learned in new situations.

At Sreenidhi, the PYP offers an inquiry-based, transdisciplinary curriculum framework that builds conceptual understanding. It is a student-centered approach to education for children aged 3–11. It reflects the best of educational research, thought leadership and experience derived from IB World Schools.

Middle Years Programme (MYP)
Ages 11–16 | Grades 6–10

The Middle Years Programme at SIS is committed to providing a supportive environment that allows students to explore and develop their own intellectual, creative and physical abilities. Students are encouraged to think critically, collaborate and learn from their experiences.

The rigorous MYP programme is built on engaging and developmentally appropriate academics, while preparing students for upper school. The programme framework encourages students to embrace and comprehend the links between their studies and the real world.

IB Diploma Programme (DP)
Ages 16–19 | Grades 11–12

The IB Diploma Programme (DP) is a rigorous, academically challenging and balanced programme designed to prepare students aged 16 to 19 for success at university and life beyond. The DP aims to encourage students to be knowledgeable, inquiring, caring and compassionate. It develops intercultural understanding, open-mindedness and the right attitude necessary to respect a range of viewpoints.

Diploma students choose six subjects from six distinct groups. At least three, and not more than four subjects are taken at Higher Level (HL) with 240 recommended teaching hours. The maximum score in Diploma is 45 points.

Group 1 — Studies in Language & Literature

  • English Language and Literature HL/SL

Group 2 — Language Acquisition

  • French B SL / French ab initio
  • Spanish B SL / Spanish ab initio
  • Hindi B SL

Group 3 — Individuals & Societies

  • Economics HL/SL
  • Business and Management HL/SL
  • Global Politics HL/SL
  • Psychology HL/SL
  • History SL

Group 4 — Sciences

  • Physics HL/SL · Chemistry HL/SL · Biology HL/SL
  • Computer Science HL/SL
  • Environmental Systems and Societies HL/SL
  • Sports, Exercise and Health Sciences SL
  • Design Technology HL/SL

Group 5 — Mathematics

  • Analysis and Approaches HL/SL
  • Applications and Interpretations HL/SL

Group 6 — The Arts

  • Visual Arts HL/SL

Three Core Elements: Extended Essay (EE) · Theory of Knowledge (TOK) · Creativity, Activity, Service (CAS) — all compulsory and central to the philosophy of the programme.

Career-Related Programme (CP / IBCP)
Ages 16–19 | Grades 11–12

The IB Career-related Programme (IBCP) is a three stage educational framework designed for students with specific career-related or vocational needs and supports higher education choices. A blend of academic subjects and Career-related study provides a holistic development for the CP graduates to pursue further education, higher education, employment or apprenticeships.

Sreenidhi International School is a recognized center by Pearson BTEC to offer International Level 3 Diploma in Business: Enterprise and Entrepreneurship and Sport: Sport, Business and Management courses.

Service Learning

Service learning connects the school-based curriculum with the natural concern of young people to care for their world. The IB programmes all approach service learning in slightly different ways, though all emphasize students developing the attributes of the IB learner profile.

Exemplary Service Activities

📖 Literacy Programme

Sreenidhians work with underprivileged children of the Aziznagar village school, helping them speak, read and write in English/Telugu with confidence.

🌿 Green Team

Creates awareness about environmental issues. Project Recycle in association with ITC — students collected waste paper for recycling.

🏥 Awareness Programmes

Sreenidhians educate housekeeping staff and drivers about the importance of hygiene and maintaining good health.

Tree Plantation

At SIS, we always incorporate life into our premises. Our children plant trees on the campus and maintain them. Regular tree plantation drives are integral to SIS — no event is complete without planting a sapling. Our one-of-a-kind organic farm inspires students to grow their own garden with love, care and passion.

Experiential Learning

Sreenidhi organises residential outdoor education trips every year for all students from PYP3 to DP2. Exchange visits with schools around the world allow students to experience different ways of living, cultures and people.
